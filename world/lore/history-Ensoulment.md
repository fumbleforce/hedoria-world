### **The Ensoulment**

Centuries ago — within living memory of the longer-lived races, well past the lives of any human — the world stood at the edge of being unmade. The great evil, \[placeholder  unmade\], was a confederation of dark powers bound together by appetite, and they were winning.

The first witch queen, **Avenoria**, performed the great working that saved the world. Drawing on every source of mana available to her — the willing output of cooperating wizards, the ambient mana of half a continent, and other things history records less clearly — she shattered the unity of the Unmade and forced soul into much of the world. The working ensouled elements (plant, metal, and others), partially redeemed some of the dark forces, and remade the moral architecture of Hedoria.

It also broke the dominance of humankind. Where humans had been the central race, the ensoulment created a continent of peoples, and humans became one race among many. They have not forgiven this. The bitterness is centuries old now, inherited rather than witnessed, but it shapes everything.

The pre-ensoulment races — those who were already people before the working — remain. The post-ensoulment races came into being through the working itself. The half-shorn lineages were partially redeemed pieces of the Unmade. The unredeemed remnants of the Unmade still exist in the deep wild and the corrupted places.
